Our College

Annasaheb Vartak College of Arts, Commerce and Science The late Padmashri Bhausaheb Vartak was a visionary par excellence and a devoted social worker. He founded the Vidyavardhini in 1970 as an institution to provide an opportunity to all students in the vicinity of Vasai an affordable quality college education which was the need of the our. Today Vidyavardhini has grown into an institute of repute with three Degree college, Engineering Degree College and B. V. Polytechnic offering Diplomas in various branches of Engineering. It recieved NAAC credential in 2003 and has proved that it is truly a space to learn, acknowledge and create dreams and watch them turn into a reality. With about 15000 students on campus, there has been immense progress in the academics, co-curricular, extracurricular and sports fields. About Nipun
"NIPUN 2023-2024: A Celebration of Traditon and Excellence"
The intercollegiate event 'NIPUN,' orchestrated by the Cultural Committee of Vatak College, stands as a multifaceted platform aimed at fostering a vibrant tapestry of objectives. Firstly, it seeks to serve as a melting pot of cultures, offering a stage where students from diverse colleges converge to celebrate, exchange, and embrace a rich mosaic of traditions and perspectives. This event is meticulously designed to unlock and exhibit the kaleidoscope of talents and creativity prevalent among the student community. Through a myriad of showcases encompassing music, dance, art, drama, literature, and more, participants are encouraged to explore their artistic prowess and express themselves freely. NIPUN, a groundbreaking college event, is set to make its debut in the academic year 2023-2024, promising a unique blend of innovation, inspiration, and progress. Designed to transcend traditional boundaries and foster a spirit of creativity, NIPUN aims to bring together students, faculty. NIPUN is not just an event; it's a movement towards a future where traditon, collaboration, and innovation are at the forefront of education and societal development.
