Our Events

Welcome to the vibrant heart of Nipun, where the pulsating rhythm of creativity takes center stage! Our annual festival is a captivating celebration that unites a spectrum of events, meticulously curated to showcase and honor the multifaceted talents within our community. Immerse yourself in the exhilarating world of Nipun as we unveil six captivating main categories, each an avenue for you to explore and revel in the richness of your diverse talents: Dance, Music, Fine Arts, Literary, Theater, and Fun Events.

Dance
Unleash your rhythm and let your passion dance through the beats! Our dance events are a fusion of style, grace, and expression. From traditional to contemporary, solo to group performances, the dance category promises a stage for every dance form which includes classical, folk and freestyle dance form.
Music
Set the melody free and make the audience groove to your tunes! Whether you're a solo artist, part of a band, or an a capella enthusiast, our music events cater to all genres and styles which includes traditional performances like classical and folk and western performances like rap and beatboxing Join us and let your harmonious journey begin!
Fine Arts
Brush, paint, and sculpt your way to artistic glory! The Fine Arts category welcomes creators of all kinds like face paintings, mehendi, on the spot painting , rangoli and tatto making. Showcase your visual prowess through paintings, sketches, sculptures, and installations. Immerse yourself in the world of colors and imagination.
Literary
Words have the power to shape worlds, and in the Literary category, we celebrate the art of storytelling and expression. Engage in riveting debates, powerful poetry, and eloquent prose with events like open mic, poetry and stand up comedy. Let your thoughts flow as you participate in a variety of literary challenges.
Theater
Lights, camera, action! Step into the spotlight with our Theater events. From gripping dramas to hilarious comedies, our stage is set for your theatrical brilliance. Bring stories to life and captivate the audience with your acting prowess with events like mono and mime.
Fun Events
Let the laughter echo and the joy multiply! The Fun Events category is all about fostering camaraderie and creating lasting memories with activities like escape room, tug of war, without fire cooking, bodybuilding, fashion show and most engaging event Mr & Ms Nipun. Engage in entertaining games, interactive challenges, and team-building activities that promise an abundance of laughter and fun.
How to Participate
  1. Visit the Registration link to sign up for your preferred events.
  2. Check the Schedule for event timings and locations.
  3. Review the Rules and Regulations from Brochure to ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience.
